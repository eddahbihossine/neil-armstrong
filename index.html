Here 's a time line of Neil Armstrong's life :

  • 1930 - Neil armstrong was born
  • 1946 - Neil loved flying things from the time he was young. At sixteen years old he earned his student pilot's license.
  • 1947 - Neil Armstrong joined the US Navy, and he attended Purdue on scholarship. He studied aeronautical engineering.
  • 1949 - Neil Armstrong was called into action with the Navy to go fight in the Korean War. When that ended, he returned to college.
  • 1952 - After returning from the war, Neil began working with the organization that would later be called NASA.
  • 1956 - Neil married a lady named Janet Shearon in 1956.
  • 1962 - In 1962, Neil moved his family to Texas so he could work with the astronaut program.
  • 1966 - Neil and his flight mate, David Scott, were launched into space to dock with the Gemini Agena vehicle. The dock was successful, but the mission had to be cut short.
  • 1969 - Neil Armstrong, along with two other astronauts, landed on the moon. Armstrong drove the Lunar Module.
  • 1969 - Armstrong and his flight mates received many awards for their moon mission. Awards included the Medal of Freedom and the Congressional Space Medal of Honor.
  • 1971 - Neil left NASA to become a professor in Ohio, at the University of Cincinnati..
  • 1971 - Neil helped investigate the failed space mission, the Challenger, in 1986. The Challenger blew up after launch, killing its crew.
  • 2006 - Neil liked to have his privacy, and he stayed out of the public eye for years. In 2006 he gave a TV interview on 60 Minutes, and later that year his biography came out.
  • 2010 - In 2010, President Barack Obama made cuts to the space program. Neil Armstrong publically spoke out against this decision.
  • 2012 - Neil died at the age of 82, after complications from heart surgery. His contribution to the space program, and our understanding of outer space, can't be measured. He will always be remembered for his giant step for mankind.
